SOUL BOWL

Phoenix, 1997. A Brunswick bowling alley flips into a hip-hop sanctuary for young dreams, loud rivalries, and one night that changes everything.

Edward Thomas · The Voice of Soul Bowl

A benchwarmer in the gym.
A headliner on the mic.

Edward is an 18-year-old MC with guarded confidence and a gift for crowd control. At Soul Bowl, his voice turns a dying Sunday night bowling alley into the city’s most charged under-21 club.

Kashuna Williams · The Heart

The calm in his storm.
The one reading the room.

Kashuna is sharp, compassionate, and street-savvy. She believes in Edward’s talent but refuses to be blinded by the spotlight. While everyone else chases the night, she’s quietly protecting the vision—and him.

West side Maryvale.
Two lines at the door.
One city inside.

Kids line up in fits they saved for all week—bowlers, dancers, ball players, and dreamers. Soul Bowl becomes the weekly ritual where under-21 Phoenix shows up to sweat, stunt, and be seen.

Rhythm · Rivalry · Fallout

One call-and-response.
One gunshot.
One night that echoes for years.

As Edward’s voice rises and the alley hits full capacity, Malcolm Champion, the fallen hometown basketball star, arrives with something to prove. When ego, envy, and bass collide, the party that built a community gets pushed to the edge.

Feature Film · Based on a true story

SOUL BOWL

A coming-of-age drama set inside a 90s hip-hop bowling night on the west side of Phoenix. Soul Bowl follows Edward, Kashuna, and Malcolm as ambition, love, and pressure collide under neon lights and a low ceiling.

Soul Bowl · Core Cast

Meet the characters

Three young lives crossing under the glow of a west side bowling alley—each pulling the night in a different direction.

The Voice

Edward Thomas

MC · Protagonist

18, confident, magnetic, and protective. Edward turns Soul Bowl into a movement with nothing but a mic, hustle, and a crew of friends trying to get out from under dead-end jobs and family pressure.

“Guarded confidence, a star in the making, tethered by loyalty.”

The Heart

Kashuna Williams

Love Interest · Confidant

18, compassionate, honest to a fault, and street savvy. Kashuna is the calm in Edward’s storm—calling out his blind spots, protecting his peace, and refusing to let the night or the fame swallow who he really is.

“The quiet strength guiding him through.”

The Force

Malcolm Champion

Power Forward · Antagonist

18, a born leader carrying the weight of expectation. After a crushing loss on the court and a future slipping away, seeing Edward rise at Soul Bowl cuts deep—turning a packed Sunday night into a pressure cooker.

“A born champion, haunted by a missed shot.”
